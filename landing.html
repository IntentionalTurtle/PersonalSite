My Past

I grew up in a larger sized suburb of Charlotte, NC called Gastonia. I was an exceptional student in terms of grades and understanding of material and graduation valedictorian at my high school. I then attended UNC Chapel Hill where I recieved a BS in Biology after four years and decided that I wanted to be a teacher. I went back to my hometown and was lucky enough to get offered a job and to teach at my high school alma mater. I was licensed as a general science expert meaning I could and did teach any and all science courses during my time. In eight years of teaching I have taught:

Anatomy and Physiology

Chemistry

Biology

Physics

Forensics

Earth and Environmental Science
